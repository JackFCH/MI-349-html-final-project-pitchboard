Stellaris fans

Short Pitch

Stellaris fans is a website for new fans to know detailed information of this game. Visitors can know the game and its company better through reading and watching in the website. There are also some hints and tutorials that teaching new fans how they could do well in the game.

Long Pitch

Stellaris is a hard but exciting PC game. There are lots of players don't know how to start, because the game itself is too complex with huge amount of data. Stellatis Fans will provide a better experience for new players who don't know where they should start. The website utilize videos, graphs, and diagram to teach beginners as well as providing some detailed data about the game itself that even be helpful to those professional players. As a result, Stellaris Fans is a website that providing all information about the game, including its publisher, history, changes, and etc.

    Introduction

    Stellaris is a 4X grand strategy video game developed and published by Paradox Interactive. Stellaris's gameplay revolves around space exploration, managing an empire, diplomacy, and space warfare with other spacefaring civilizations. It was released worldwide for Windows, OS X, and Linux on May 9, 2016,[1] and was released on February 26, 2019 for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
